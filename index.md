I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Economics, University of Turku, Finland, and lab manager of the UTU Choice Lab.

My research interests are in behavioral and experimental public economics, with a particular focus on the consequences of income inequalities and differential access to health services.

I am affiliated with the [Finnish Centre of Excellence in Tax Systems Research (FIT)](https://verotutkimus.fi/en/home/), the [Finnish Research Infrastructure for Public Opinion (FIRIPO)](https://www.firipo.fi), the [INVEST Research Flagship Center](https://invest.utu.fi/), and [Helsinki LABBET](https://www.helsinkilabbet.fi/).
